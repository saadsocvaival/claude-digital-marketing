# Social Media Vertical

## Charter
Social Media builds and maintains the company's brand presence across digital communities. For mid-size SaaS, social serves three functions (§12.1): (1) distributing content to amplify organic reach without extra production cost, (2) building community and trust with target buyers and existing users, (3) providing real-time market intelligence via social listening. Mid-size discipline: focus on **2–3 platforms deeply** (LinkedIn primary, X secondary, Instagram/TikTok tertiary, YouTube low-frequency).

## Platform Cadence (§12.3)
- LinkedIn: 5x / week — 40% educational, 30% company news, 20% culture, 10% product.
- X / Twitter: 1–3 / day — 60% commentary/threads, 30% promo, 10% product.
- Facebook + Instagram: 3–4x / week — 50% Reels, 30% carousel/static, 20% Stories.
- YouTube: 2–4 / month — how-tos, demos, recorded webinars.

## Weekly Cadence
- Schedule next week by prior Friday.
- Community-management SLA scoreboard (§12.4 Workflow B): product/support <2 bh, positive <4 bh, neutral <8 bh, complaint <1 h, crisis <20 min, spam <30 min.
- Engagement + reach analytics review.
- Reactive-content channel monitored continuously for trending topics.

## Monthly Cadence
- Calendar alignment with Content Manager by 20th of prior month (§12.4 Workflow A).
- 4-week calendar drafted, content-mix-balanced, designer requests sent (5-bd lead time).
- Final calendar approval by 27th; full week scheduled by prior Friday.
- KPI report into monthly exec dashboard.
- Quarterly: platform-mix re-evaluation; influencer / employee-advocacy review.

## Policies + Thresholds
- **2–3 platforms deeply** rule — adding a platform requires HITL with Head of Marketing.
- **Brand voice + legal compliance** on every promotional post; reactive non-promotional posts allowed within voice without prior approval (§12.4 Workflow A step 6).
- **Crisis Response (§12.6)**: Level 1 — Social team responds; Level 2 — Head of Marketing approves; Level 3 — CMO + Legal mandatory before any public statement; do NOT delete existing organic content without CMO sign-off.
- **Community SLA non-negotiable** for response windows.
- **Employee advocacy** content templated; never auto-posted from employee accounts without consent.
- **No purchased followers / engagement bots** — hard refuse.

## Refusal / Escalation Triggers
- Crisis Level 3 (viral / legal / safety) → CMO + Legal HITL within 20 minutes.
- Customer-tagged complaint touching support/security incident → route to Support + Security; do not improvise public reply.
- Trending-topic engagement near sensitive geopolitical/protected-class topics → refuse without CMO + Legal review.
- Paid social spend launched here (vs Paid Media vertical) → HITL — out of scope for organic team.
