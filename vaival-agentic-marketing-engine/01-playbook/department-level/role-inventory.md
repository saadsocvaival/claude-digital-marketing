# Role Inventory

Authoritative inventory of every role named in the playbook (`01-playbook/source/Final-Playbook-Mid-Size-Team-Marketing-Department.md`). Tier mapping:

- **Tier 1** — Founder / CEO
- **Tier 2** — CMO, Head of Digital Marketing
- **Tier 3** — Vertical Manager (one per vertical)
- **Tier 4** — Specialist / Senior Specialist / Lead-level individual contributor
- **Tier 5** — Coordinator / Junior / Executive / Writer / Designer / Analyst under a specialist or manager

## Department-Level Roles

| Role | Tier | Reports To | Playbook Section | Key Responsibilities |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Founder / CEO | tier-1 | — | §3.1, §4.2 | Final authority on strategy, budget envelope, crisis escalation |
| Chief Marketing Officer (CMO) | tier-2 | Founder / CEO | §3.1, §4.2, §5.3 | Brand, messaging, strategy approval, over-$15k spend approval, crisis comms |
| Head of Digital Marketing | tier-2 | CMO | §3.1, §4.1, §5.3, §5.4 | Department leadership, budget ownership, vertical lead management, campaign approval $3k–$15k, headcount |

## Cross-Functional / Shared Notes

- **Shared roles** per §3.1 "Mid-Size Reality": Content + SEO (writers own on-page SEO execution), Analytics + CRM (Marketing Ops may own CRM data + attribution), Social + Content (short-form creators span both), Web + CRO (two-person team).
- **Cross-functional partners named in the playbook (§3.2)** but not in-scope for this department hierarchy: Sales, Product/Professional Services, Customer Success, Finance/CEO, Legal.

## Ambiguity Resolutions

1. **CRO Specialist** appears in both Paid Media (§10.2) and Website Development (§15.2). Placed under Website Development as primary owner; cross-listed as shared with Paid Media.
2. **Marketing Automation Specialist** appears in both Email & CRM (§13.2) and Analytics & Ops (§14.2). Placed under Email & CRM as primary, cross-listed as shared with Marketing Ops.
3. **Technical SEO + AEO Specialist** is tagged "Shared" with Website Development; placed under SEO as primary owner.
4. **GEO Monitoring** listed as "Shared / Rotating" — modeled as a tier-5 coordinator role rotating among SEO team members.
5. **Content Editor** not in the §11.2 team composition table but named in §5.2, §8.2, §11.4 workflows; modeled as a tier-4 role under Content Manager.
6. **Tier-5 roles** were derived from workflow actors and Appendix B hiring roadmap, since the playbook's role tables stop at specialist. Each tier-5 role cites the workflow section that names its activities.
