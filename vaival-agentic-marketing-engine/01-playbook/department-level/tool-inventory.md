## Ambiguity Resolutions

1. **HubSpot** appears in three distinct capacities (CRM, Marketing Hub, attribution). Modeled as three separate connector stubs to reflect distinct API surfaces and approval scopes.
2. **SEMrush vs. Ahrefs** — playbook says "primary or" but both are listed; both included as separate connectors since §9.4 notes "if budget allows both; else pick one".
3. **Mailchimp / Klaviyo** listed as alternatives to HubSpot Email. Both included as separate connectors.
4. **Webflow vs. WordPress** — alternatives. Both retained.
5. **VWO, AB Tasty, "Google Optimize successor"** — retained VWO + AB Tasty. "Google Optimize successor" is too unspecified for its own connector stub.
6. **ChatGPT/Claude/Gemini/Perplexity/Copilot** — grouped under `ai-enablement/` category since they are used manually (not via API) for GEO audits.
7. **G2 / Capterra / Trustpilot** — classified under `data-attribution/` since they function as citation sources feeding attribution/GEO signal, not standard marketing tools.
