## 1. Meta
- **Campaign name:** GS_FinOps_Platform_Q3
- **Channel:** Google Search Network only.
- **Owner:** Paid Media DRI.
- **Status:** draft.
- **Launch:** within 5 BD of account approval.

## 2. Objective
- **Primary:** `demo_request` on `/aws` (via FinOps generic intent funneling into AWS-first ICP).
- **Secondary:** `cta_click` to `/blog/ri-vs-savings-plans`, newsletter signups via content upgrade.

## 3. ICP & Audience
- Same ICP. Looser query intent → tighter audience layering.
- **Audience layering (observation + bid mod):**
  - In-market: Enterprise software, Cloud computing — bid +0%.
  - Customer Match: seed lists — bid +30%.
  - Site visitors last 30 days — bid +50% (if data permits).
- **Geos:** US, CA, UK, IE, AU, NZ.
- **Devices:** desktop +10%, mobile −15%.

## 4. Hypothesis
> Practitioners searching "finops platform" / "cloud cost optimization" without AWS in the query are still 70%+ AWS-first; routing them to `/aws` will convert at ≥ 3% LP→demo_request with CPA ≤ $300.

## 5. Budget
- **Monthly seed:** $1,000 ($33/day).
- **Bid:** Maximize Conversions for 21 days; switch to tCPA $300 at ≥ 25 conversions.
