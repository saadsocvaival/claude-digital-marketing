## Tone rules

- First person ("I", "we"), never corporate.
- No emojis unless the founder uses emojis natively elsewhere.
- One CTA per post.
- Always include the URL.
- Reply rate: founder replies to every top-level comment in the first 6 hours. Sets engagement baseline.
