# Employee Advocacy Rollout — Q3/Q4 2026

## Phase 1: Months 1-2 (Founder + 1 marketing op)
**Volume:** ~5 posts/week each (10 total/week)
**Cadence:** Mon LinkedIn long-form / Tue X thread / Wed quick-take / Thu carousel re-share / Fri customer-or-product update

**Founder:** thought leadership + behind-the-scenes
**Marketing op:** company-page amplification + community engagement

## Phase 2: Months 3-4 (+ 5 team members)
Add: 5 employees from engineering/CS/sales who opted in.
**Volume:** 2 posts/week each = +10/week (20 total/week)
**Content menu:** 5 ready-to-share posts/week, each tailored to their role:
- **Engineering:** "what we shipped this week" type posts
- **CS:** customer-outcome stories (anonymised)
- **Sales:** "objection of the week" + how it was answered

**Training:** 30-min LinkedIn voice/safety session per joiner.

## Phase 3: Months 5-6 (full team)
**Volume:** target 2 posts/week per employee = full company amplification.
**Content menu:** ~15 ready-to-share posts/week from a shared library
**Tools:** Buffer for queueing; Slack `#advocacy` channel for daily prompts

## Sharing rules

- ALL employee posts: their voice, not company copy. Encourage editing.
- 3 hashtags max (per LinkedIn algorithm guidance)
- Always disclose employer (avoid PR/legal issues)
- Don't share confidential customer info ever
- Don't make claims about competitors that the company itself wouldn't make
- Run anything that mentions specific revenue/customer numbers through marketing first

## Incentives

Phase 1-2: peer recognition only.
Phase 3: quarterly $500 spot-bonus for top 3 organic-reach posts. Public recognition in all-hands.

## Measurement

Track per-employee:
- Posts per week
- Total reach + engagement
- Inbound conversations (DMs, comments) generated
- Net follower delta
- Conversion-attributable signals (used for revenue contribution)

Report weekly in V7 weekly digest.

## What we don't do

- ❌ Mandate employee posts (legal + culture nightmare)
- ❌ Ghost-write employee posts
- ❌ Use bot accounts or fake amplification
- ❌ Penalise opt-outs

## Why this matters

Founder + marketing-op alone can sustain a brand presence; full-team advocacy is what makes a brand visible in feeds at scale. CostSage's competitors (CloudZero, nOps) have 30-100+ employees and use this routinely. Eventually we will too.
