# Announcement Templates (3)

For company milestones that are *news*, not promotions. Audience: customers + newsletter subscribers + investors/partners (where appropriate).

UTM: `?utm_source=email&utm_medium=announce&utm_campaign=<campaign>&utm_content=<variant>`

---

## Template 1 — Company Milestone

**Use case:** crossing $X ARR, hitting Nth customer, anniversary, major hire.

**Subject A:** A milestone for CostSage
**Subject B:** {{milestone_short}} — and what's next
**Preview text:** Short note; no ask.

**Body:**

{{first_name}},

A quick milestone note: {{milestone_one_sentence}}.

**Why it matters:**
{{two_to_three_sentence_context}}

**What's next:**
{{one_paragraph_what_changes}}

**Thanks to:**
{{customers, team, partners — short, sincere}}

The work continues. If you've been part of this — replying to support, sending product feedback, telling a colleague — thank you. It's not a small thing.

**One CTA →** {{cta_label}}: {{cta_url}}?utm_source=email&utm_medium=announce&utm_campaign={{slug}}&utm_content=A

— {{sender_first}}
Founder, CostSage

## Operational notes

- Announcements are *news* tone — not marketing tone. No emoji, no exclamation marks, no superlatives.
- Customers should always hear announcements *before* the public press cycle. Stagger send: customer segment T-0, public newsletter T+24h.
- Hiring announcements suppressed for contacts who opted out of "company updates" (separate consent toggle from newsletter / promos).
- Compliance footer auto-appended.
