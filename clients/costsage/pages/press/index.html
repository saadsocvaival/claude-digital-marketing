300-word version

CostSage is the agentic AI FinOps platform that closes the gap between cloud-cost visibility and actual savings. While most FinOps tools surface waste in dashboards and leave engineering teams to act on it manually, CostSage's AI agents reason about your cloud environment, plan optimisations, and execute them under operator approval — turning recurring waste reports into real bill reductions.

The platform identifies and applies five categories of optimisation that traditional dashboards leave on the table: VM and container rightsizing across AWS and Azure; Reserved Instance and Savings Plan portfolio modelling and execution; idle-resource detection and cleanup; tagging-governance enforcement at provisioning time; and storage-tier optimisation. Every action is approval-gated, audit-trailed, and reversible. The platform is built for AWS-first SaaS teams with $10K–$500K monthly cloud spend who have outgrown raw cost-explorer dashboards but aren't ready for enterprise consulting engagements.
