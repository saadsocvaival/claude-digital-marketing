Security & Responsible Disclosure

CostSage runs an open responsible-disclosure programme. If you've found a vulnerability, please follow the path below — we treat reports seriously and respond fast.

Security contact: security@costsage.ai
Disclosure URL: https://costsage.ai/.well-known/security.txt
PGP key: [TBD-OPERATOR — to be published at /.well-known/pgp-key.txt]
Response SLA: initial acknowledgement within 3 business days

Reporting

Email security@costsage.ai with: a clear description of the issue, reproduction steps, the affected URL or component, and your preferred contact method. If the issue is sensitive, encrypt with our PGP key (link above).

Scope

The following are in scope for our programme:

  • costsage.ai and its subdomains
  • The CostSage product (web app + APIs)
  • AWS Marketplace listing infrastructure when reachable from the public internet

Out of scope

  • Findings from automated scanners that depend on social engineering of CostSage staff
  • Issues affecting third-party services we don't operate (e.g., the LLM platforms we link to)
  • Theoretical attacks without proof-of-concept
  • Reports on missing security headers without a demonstrable exploit (we publish our header set transparently)
  • UI / cosmetic issues without a security impact

Our commitments

  • Acknowledgement: within 3 business days of report
  • Triage: within 7 business days for severity classification
  • Fix or mitigation: within 30 days for high/critical, 90 days for medium, prioritised for low
  • Coordinated disclosure: we work with you on disclosure timing
  • Recognition: at your request, we credit you on this page after fix

Reward statement
