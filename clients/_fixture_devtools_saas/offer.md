# Offer Architecture — Loopgate

## 1. Core Offer
- **Team plan**: $25/user/mo, min 3 seats, self-serve, 14-day trial, no credit card.
- **Business plan**: $99/user/mo, SSO/SCIM, SOC2 report, priority support, min 10 seats.
- **Enterprise**: custom; self-host option, 99.99% SLA, dedicated CSM, contract term ≥1yr, min $50k ACV.
- **Free tier**: up to 3 users, 5 flags, community support — serves as qualified lead source.

## 2. Offer Ladder

| Services | Migration concierge | $5k one-time, waived at $50k+ ACV | LD migrators | time-to-cutover |

## 3. Commercial Levers
- Annual prepay: 15% discount.
- Startup program: 50% off year 1 if raised <Series B and ≤50 eng.
- Migration credit: up to $5k off year 1 for teams migrating from LD or a documented homegrown system.
- Volume: 100/250/500-seat tiers with 10%/15%/20% discount.
- No price decoys; pricing page is public.

## 4. Non-Negotiables
- No perpetual discounts.
- No custom contracts under $50k ACV.
- No feature gating on audit log (audit is core, not upsell).
- No exclusivity/MSA lock on OSS components.
