# Landing Page Brief — /lp/flag-tco-calc

> Rubric: `/rubrics/landing-page.yaml`.

## 1. Single Conversion Goal
Complete TCO calculator → email capture on results screen. Secondary: demo request.

## 2. Source & Message Match
Sources: Google search (Ad Group "TCO-Shock"), LinkedIn Variant A, Reddit, pillar blog CTAs.
Hero headline matches ad variant A: "Your flag table is costing $132k/yr — see yours".

## 3. Wireframe
1. **Hero** — H1: "How much is your homegrown flag system really costing?" Sub: "2-min calculator. No form to start. Based on interviews with 40 platform teams." Primary CTA: "Start the calc". Social proof strip: 8 logos.
2. **Calculator (inline)** — inputs: eng-count, current flag system, deploy cadence, compliance posture. Output: per-year cost (low/mid/high), audit risk band, recommended next step.
3. **Result screen** — reveal + email capture for "send this as a PDF + send the full methodology". Fields: email + eng-count (pre-filled).
4. **How we got to these numbers** — methodology: interview count, assumptions, links to raw data.
5. **Social proof** — 3 customer quotes with name + title + logo.
6. **Comparison snippet** — "If these numbers made you reach for a vendor: here's what Loopgate costs at your size."
7. **Objection block** — "But we'd rather build it" / "LD seems safer" / "Migration sounds scary" — each with one-paragraph counter.
8. **Final CTA** — "Talk to a platform engineer on our team" (booking link) + "Or start free (3 users, 5 flags)".
9. **Minimal footer** — trust badges (SOC2 in audit, OSS repo), privacy link, unsub.

No nav bar. No unrelated links.

## 4. Copy
Voice: per `brand-voice.md`. Reading level Flesch 55. No "revolutionary", no "seamless".

## 5. Form
Fields: email, eng-count (pre-filled from calc), role (optional). Justification: email for delivery; eng-count for personalization of follow-up; role for routing. No phone — friction > value here.

## 6. Mobile
Hero + "Start the calc" button above fold at 375px. Calc inputs vertical-stack. Tap targets ≥44px.

## 7. Experiment Hypothesis
"We believe a dollar-pain-led hero + inline calculator (vs form-gated PDF) will lift email-capture CVR from baseline 8% to 14% because the interactive calc creates reciprocity and surfaces personalized pain. Success = ≥12% CVR over 3,000 sessions with 95% confidence."

## 8. Measurement
- Primary: calc-complete → email-capture CVR.
- Micro: calc-start rate, calc-complete rate, results-scroll depth, demo-CTA click rate.
- Heatmap + session replay on first 200 sessions.
- A/B: variant B with "SOC2 audit" hero vs Variant A (dollar hero). N ≥ 3,000 per arm.
