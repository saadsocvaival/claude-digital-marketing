# Email Sequence — Winback (Churned Paid + Stalled Trials)

Goal: 6% reactivation on churned Team/Business within 60d of cancel.
Entry: cancelled paid plan OR trial expired without conversion in last 60d. Exit: reactivate OR 3 emails sent OR unsub.

## E1 — T+7 days after cancel — "No pitch — what didn't work?"
- Subject: "What didn't work for you?"
- From: Rae (human signature)
- Body: 2 lines. "We don't get real feedback unless we ask. If you have 30 seconds — what pushed you off Loopgate? Reply, or pick one: [too expensive / missing feature / chose competitor / didn't need / other]."
- CTA: Reply.

## E2 — T+21 days — "Here's what's new"
- Subject: "Things that shipped since you left"
- Body: Changelog of 3 notable shipments. No soft-sell. Link to public changelog.
- CTA: See changelog.

## E3 — T+45 days — Re-offer
- Subject: "Winback: 30 days on us, if timing is right"
- Body: 30-day free credit on whichever plan they had. One-click reactivation.
- CTA: Reactivate with credit.

## Measurement
- Reactivations at 60/90/180d.
- Churn reason capture rate (E1 reply rate target ≥15%).
- Deliverability guardrails — bounce <1%, complaint <0.1%.
