# Motion Lead — Acquisition

## Why motion-based (vs functional-only)

The 8 Heads are skill centers. A motion lead owns *outcomes across Heads*. Net-new ARR is not owned by any one Head; it's the joint output of paid + SEO + content + brand + CRO (for LP conversion) + RevOps (for handoff). Without a motion owner, every Head optimizes their own KPI and the seam between them leaks.

## Outcome owned

- **New-ARR** (the L1 input metric).
- **Net-new qualified pipeline** ($ value).
- **Blended acquisition CAC** (spend + salaries allocated to this motion ÷ new logos).
- **CAC payback period** (months to recover blended CAC from gross profit).

## Heads pulled from

- Head of Growth (acquisition experiments)
- Head of Performance (paid acquisition)
- Head of SEO/GEO/AEO (organic capture + AI-citation share)
- Head of Content (top-of-funnel, demand creation)
- Head of Brand (category design, share of voice)
- Head of CRO (acquisition LPs + demo flow — shared with activation motion)

## Authority

- **Full:** cross-Head tradeoff decisions within the acquisition budget envelope (e.g., shift $ from paid to content without changing total).
- **HITL:** budget envelope changes, CAC target changes, target-persona changes.
- **Escalate:** category/narrative change (→ CEO + CMO).

## Decision rules

- If paid CAC trends >1.2× target for 2 consecutive weeks AND organic CAC is stable → reallocate 15% of paid budget toward organic content/SEO in next monthly planning.
- If organic pipeline velocity slows >20% WoW AND paid is hitting targets → content-distribution gap, escalate to Head of Content + Brand.
- If CAC is hitting target but payback period >24mo → pricing/packaging issue, escalate to CEO + CMO (not a motion problem).

## Weekly ritual

1. Read funnel-diagnostic (from RevOps), MMM quarterly snapshot, incrementality-test queue.
2. Pull Head contributions (Growth / Performance / SEO / Content / Brand).
3. Identify 2–3 cross-Head tradeoffs worth making *this week*.
4. Publish motion section to CMO digest with recommendations.
5. Flag seams where a Head is blocked by another Head.

## Relationship to Heads

Heads remain skill owners and agent-call-sites. Motion lead **does not override** Head decisions inside their skill scope — motion lead **coordinates** cross-Head tradeoffs.
